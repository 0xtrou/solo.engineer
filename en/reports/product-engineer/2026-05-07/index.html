5. Vietnam EPR Compliance Tool — New BUILD

Nghị định 110/2026/NĐ-CP, just published on May 7, replaces the entire EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) framework with mandatory recycling targets for packaging, electronics, batteries, and vehicles. This is a compliance shock: companies must establish or contract with formal recycling channels, maintain auditable waste tracking records, and submit periodic compliance reports. The vast majority of Vietnamese manufacturers and importers lack the infrastructure to comply.

A 30-day build would target the simplest compliance need: an EPR reporting and tracking SaaS that helps companies calculate mandatory recycling percentages by product category, track recycling partnerships, and generate compliance reports in the format required by the decree. Stack: Next.js + Supabase with Vietnamese-language interface, Momo/ZaloPay integration for subscription billing. Revenue model: $50–200/month per company, with potential for audit-ready report generation as a premium add-on. Market size is significant: Vietnam has thousands of manufacturers and importers in affected categories, with no existing domestic compliance tool. Top risk: government may provide free compliance tooling or delay enforcement.
