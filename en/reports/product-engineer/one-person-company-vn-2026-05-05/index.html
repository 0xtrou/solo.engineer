Executive Summary

Resolution 86/NQ-CP introduces a pilot "one-person enterprise" (doanh nghiệp một người) model — a fundamentally new legal form in Vietnam distinct from the existing single-member LLC (Công ty TNHH 1 thành viên). Combined with streamlined bankruptcy, regulatory sandboxes, and IP-backed credit guarantees, this creates a greenfield opportunity for legal-tech products targeting solo entrepreneurs. The window is 6–18 months before implementing decrees land, making now the ideal time to build positioning and MVP.

Verdict: High-opportunity, low-competition niche. A solo developer can realistically capture first-mover advantage with a focused MVP targeting incorporation assistance + compliance tracking. Addressable market: 500K–1M potential solo entrepreneurs.

  • The gap: most operate as "hộ kinh doanh cá thể" (individual business household) or stay completely informal because formal company registration is too burdensome

1.2 Current Barriers to Single-Member LLC (Công ty TNHH 1TV)

Under the Enterprise Law 2020 (Luật Doanh nghiệp 2020), the single-member LLC exists but has significant practical barriers:

  1. Accounting & Compliance Overhead: Must maintain full double-entry accounting, file quarterly VAT, monthly tax declarations, annual financial statements — even if revenue is minimal
  2. Minimum Capital Requirements: No statutory minimum, but banks and licensing authorities often require demonstrated capital for certain business lines
  3. Legal Persona Requirements: Must have a legal representative, registered office address (not PO Box), company seal, charter capital documentation
  4. Banking: Opening a corporate bank account requires full registration, and many banks have minimum balance requirements
  5. Tax Complexity: Corporate income tax (20%), VAT (8-10%), personal income tax for the owner — dual taxation effectively
  6. Cost: Typical cost to set up through a law firm: 3–5M VND ($120–$200), plus ongoing accounting service: 1–3M VND/month ($40–$120)
  7. Dissolution Complexity: Closing a company takes 5–6 months minimum, requires tax clearance, debt settlement, public notices

Result: For a freelancer earning 10–30M VND/month ($400–$1,200), the overhead of a formal LLC is prohibitive. They stay as "hộ kinh doanh" or operate entirely informally.

1.3 What the New Model Likely Changes

Based on the policy direction of 86/NQ-CP and comparative models (Japan's 個人事業主 → 会社制度 simplification, UK's sole trader → limited company, France's auto-entrepreneur):

  • Simplified Registration: Expected to be registerable online with minimal paperwork, potentially through National Business Registration Portal
  • Reduced Compliance: Likely exempt from full double-entry accounting; simplified tax regime probable
  • Limited Liability: Key differentiator from hộ kinh doanh — personal asset protection
  • Streamlined Bankruptcy: Resolution specifically mentions simplified bankruptcy — easier to wind down
  • IP as Capital: Can use intellectual property as registered capital — crucial for tech/freelance startups
  • Lower Tax Burden: May qualify for startup tax incentives under the national strategy framework
  • Regulatory Sandbox Access: Access to pilot programs for testing innovative business models
