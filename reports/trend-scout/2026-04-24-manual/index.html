📊 Key Patterns

1. The "Skills for Agents" Meta-Trend

The single biggest trend across Trendshift. CLAUDE.md, agent skills collections, skill managers, marketing skills, design skills — the ecosystem is self-organizing around reusable, composable capabilities for AI coding agents. This is npm/crates.io for AI agents.

2. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the New SEO

Multiple TrustMRR companies ($50-73k MRR each) are built around optimizing for AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overviews). This category didn't exist 18 months ago and now has 5+ companies at $50k+ MRR.

3. Vertical AI SaaS > Horizontal AI SaaS

The highest-revenue TrustMRR companies aren't general AI tools — they're AI applied to specific verticals: healthcare (TrimRx, Kibu), creator commerce (Supliful), legal/compliance, real estate. Vertical wins.

4. Open Source is Eating Agent Infrastructure

Langfuse (observability), RAG-Anything, code search MCP, headless browser — core agent infrastructure is being open-sourced. The money is in the application layer on top.

5. Stealth/Unnamed Companies Dominate High MRR

#2 ($381k MRR) and #10 ($183k MRR) on TrustMRR are unnamed/stealth. The most profitable AI businesses are still in stealth — public leaderboards only show the tip of the iceberg.
