Conclusions

Top Trends (Cross-Platform)

  1. AI Agent Skills & Frameworks dominate GitHub — Over 60% of trending repos are AI agent configurations, skills, or frameworks. The meta-layer (teaching AI how to work) matters more than the AI itself.

  2. Claude Code Ecosystem is exploding — Multiple top-25 repos are Claude Code skills, configs, and tools. This is the fastest-growing developer ecosystem right now.

  3. AI Sales & Marketing Automation prints money — DM Champ ($190K), PROSP ($128K), SeoBOT ($75K), Cometly ($214K) — AI selling and marketing is the highest-revenue category.

  4. Health tech MRR is quietly massive — Stan ($3.6M), TrimRx ($323K), Kibu ($234K), Medical Practice ($149K) — healthcare/telehealth dominates the revenue charts.

  5. Self-hosted & privacy-first sees sustained demand — RustDesk (112k ⭐), paperless-ngx (38.4k ⭐), thunderbolt — open-source alternatives to commercial tools keep growing.

Key insight: The gap between GitHub hype and actual revenue is enormous. Projects with massive star counts generate zero direct revenue, while unsexy vertical SaaS in healthcare and sales automation quietly generates millions.

Opportunities

  1. AI Agent Configuration as a Service — Everyone's making CLAUDE.md files and skills. Nobody's selling a curated, tested, maintained collection as a subscription. The "dotfiles for AI" market.

  2. Vertical AI Sales Agents — DM Champ proves AI can close $40K treatments. Apply the same pattern to other verticals: real estate, legal, consulting, B2B SaaS. White-label AI closer.

  3. GLP-1 Adjacent Products — TrimRx at $323K MRR with 18% growth. The weight-loss drug economy needs companion tools: nutrition tracking, side-effect management, progress analytics.

  4. I/DD & Specialized Care Tech — Kibu at 53% growth in an underserved niche. Applied to other specialized care verticals (autism support, elderly care, chronic disease management).

  5. SEO for AI/LLM Visibility — SeoBOT and AEO Engine both focus on LLM visibility. This is a new category — making brands discoverable by ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc. First-mover advantage.

Quick Takes

  • Claude Code is the new VS Code. The ecosystem around it (skills, configs, frameworks) is where all developer energy is flowing. If you're building for devs, build for Claude Code.

  • "AI selling to humans" is the highest-ROI application. Not code generation, not art — autonomous sales agents closing high-ticket deals. DM Champ's pitch says it all: "AI sells like your best closer, 24/7."

  • Healthcare MRR is underserved and lucrative. The top revenue list is dominated by health plays, yet GitHub trending has zero health projects. Massive opportunity gap between developer attention and where the money flows.
